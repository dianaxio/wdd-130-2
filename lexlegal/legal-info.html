Legal Info

Lex Legal is your one stop shop for all things lawful. We are committed to making sure Legal assistants have what they need to succeed in helping you. Throughout our site, you will find multiple resources to find solutions.

The career

The career began to develop in the late 1960s when law firms and individual practitioners sought ways to improve legal services' efficient and cost-effective delivery. Utilization of legal assistants in a law firm ultimately reduces the cost to the client and frees time for lawyers.

Assistans Qualified

Legal assistants are qualified by education, training, or work experience and are employed or retained by a lawyer, law office, corporation, governmental agency, or other entity to perform specifically delegated substantive legal work for which a lawyer is responsible. In a law firm setting, assistant legals time spent on substantive legal work is billed to clients at market rates, similar to other professional staff, but at a lower rate. This distinguishes legal assistants from other non-lawyer staff members.
