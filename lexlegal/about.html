About Us

Since 2000, Lex Legal has been trusted to provide exceptional continuing education and professional development designed specifically to advance legal assistants and other legal professionals in their careers. Lex Legal connects members with their peers across the nation and opens doors of knowledge and opportunity.

Leading Force

Some things in the legal profession—like smart, sustainable growth—take time and care. Our sales-minded lawyer virtual receptionists or legal virtual assistants engage potential clients with empathy and commitment. Trained to carefully execute outbound call campaigns aimed at nurturing qualified leads, reviving lost prospects, and following up with potential clients, our lead-generating specialists in the lawyer answering service have your firms voice and values in mind, navigating the client conversion process with sensitivity and patience.

Lex Legal provides current information about the legal assistant profession through continuing education, networking opportunities, professional certification programs, occupational survey reports, and other publications to help legal assistants excel in the workplace. Lex Legal is an organization representing more than 12k+ Legal Assistants who are individual members or members of Lex Legal affiliated associations. Lex Legal is a membership organization. This means that the individual members of Lex Legal are the leading force of Lex Legal programs and policies. The association is governed by a Board of Directors and maintains an office and headquarters staff in Rosario, Argentina.
